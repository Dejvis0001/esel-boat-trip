OUR WORLD IS OUT THERE!

Set sail with Esel Boat Trip for an unforgettable maritime journey. Cruise along stunning coastlines, uncover hidden gems, and embrace the serene beauty of the open sea.

Our trips are crafted to offer the perfect balance of travel and exploration. Enjoy roughly an hour of scenic cruising, with opportunities to admire marine life along the way. Upon arrival at your destination, you'll have approximately 3.5 hours to relax and soak up the surroundings. Whether you're snorkeling, sunbathing, or simply taking in the tranquil atmosphere, the choice is yours.

For added excitement, try our tubing experiences and create lasting memories with friends and family. Ready to embark on your adventure? Book with Esel Boat Trip today and let us show you the wonders of the ocean in a whole new light!
