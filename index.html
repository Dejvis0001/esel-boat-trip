A Few About Us

Explore the beauty of the seas with Esel Boat Trip. Cruise along coastlines, discover hidden coves, and embrace the tranquility of the open water.

With safety as our priority, our knowledgeable pilots ensure smooth sailing for every trip. Whether you seek adventure with tubing or relaxation with sunset cruises, we cater to all preferences.

Trips typically last 1 hour, with 3.5 hours at the destination for exploration and relaxation. For an extra thrill, try our tubing experiences.

Ready to set sail? Book your adventure with Esel Boat Trip today!

Ushtari Beach

Ushtari Beach, situated northeast of Saranda and across from Kakome Beach, is a small beach with large stones and lush greenery. It can be reached by boat or on foot.

Gremina Beach
