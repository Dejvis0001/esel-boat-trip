Explore our Destinations In South Saranda!

Explore South Saranda's stunning coastlines, from the secluded Shpella e Pëllumbave beach to the enchanting Three Ksamil Islands.
Discover the hidden beauty and serene ambiance of Tango Island.

Shpella e Pëllumbave is a small beach nestled between the rocks of the Ionian coast near Ksamil, in southern Albania. It is located right next to Mirror Beach..

Shpella E Pellumbave

In front of Ksamil's main beach are three uninhabited islands in Butrint National Park. Known for their turquoise waters and sandy beaches, these islands offer a paradise-like escape.

Three Ksamil Islands

Tongo Island is an uninhabited, 2.5-hectare rocky island in the Ionian Sea between Albania and Greece, just 300 meters from the Greek coast, featuring a beautiful sandy beach and rich aquatic life.

Tango Island

Explore our Destinations in North Sarada!

Uncover North Saranda's natural wonders, from the sheltered Gjiri I Roidhes with its vibrant reefs to the picturesque Ushtari Beach. Enjoy the tranquil beauty of Grammata Bay on the Karaburun Peninsula.

In Rrojdhe, where sailors seek refuge from rough seas, the dense underwater vegetation adds a unique color and clarity to the water. The reefs are teeming with sea urchins, so stepping carelessly might lead to spending the rest of your trip removing spines from your feet.

Gjiri I Roidhes

Ushtari Beach, situated northeast of Saranda and across from Kakome Beach, is a small beach with large stones and lush greenery. It can be reached by boat or on foot.

Ushtari Beach

Gremina Beach, situated on the Karaburun Peninsula, lies near Orikum on the western slope of the peninsula in Pashaliman.

Gremina Beach

Krorëza Beach, located on the Albanian Riviera just above Kakoma Bay, is a secluded spot surrounded by rocks on three sides and featuring stunning turquoise waters.

Kroreza
